 HISTORY

The first KoYon-jeon was held 25th December, 1945. For 2 days, only soccer game was held. After 1 year, in 1946, KoYon-jeon started to have additional baseball, basketball, and hockey matches. There were several historic events that stopped the KoYon-jeon. However, from 1965, it continued its matches until today.
